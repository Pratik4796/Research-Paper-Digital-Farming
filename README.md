## 📌 Title

## 📚 Published In

## 📅 Publication Date

---

## 📝 Abstract
This research paper focuses on the transformation of traditional farming into digital agriculture.  
It highlights the major challenges faced by farmers, government policies, and the benefits of adopting modern technologies.

---

## 🚀 Key Highlights
- Transition from traditional to digital farming  
- Role of technology in agriculture  
- Government policies and support  
- Benefits for farmers and productivity
